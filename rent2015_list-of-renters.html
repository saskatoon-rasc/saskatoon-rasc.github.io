Saskatoon Centre RASC TELESCOPE LOANER PROGRAM

Since August 1996, Saskatoon Centre has made its telescopes available for rent to RASC members on a monthly basis. We have six telescopes available for rent:

  1. 3.1 inch Tasco refractor
  2. 4 inch Astroscan compact reflector
  3. 6 inch rich field reflector
  4. Meade 8 inch skywatcher - telescope 1
  5. Meade 8 inch skywatcher - telescope 2
  6. Meade 8 inch skywatcher - telescope 3
RULES:
The telescopes normally should be picked up and returned to our current telescope curator, Errol Frazer-Harrison, at the Observatory on the University of Saskatchewan campus. Here are the rental rates and rules:
  1. Cost is $10 per calendar month; $5 for less than half a calendar month.

    Example 1: pick up on October 12, return November 5, cost = $15.

    Example 2: pick up October 10, return October 31, cost = $10.

    Example 3: pick up October 12, return November 20, cost = $20. (The best deal is to use for 15 days completely within a 31 day month like October, cost = $5.)

  2. To arrange for a telescope rental please e-mail our current telescope curator (see above) with the following information: your name, the scope you want or have, when you want it or when you want to bring it back.

  3. If you are poor or think that the rental fees are outragous, please borrow the telescope anyway and pay what you can.

  4. You can make your payment when you return the `scope, please make cheques payable to "Saskatoon Centre RASC".

  5. You may keep the `scope for a minimum of one month. So if someone else wants it before you've had it for a month, I will contact you and ask you to return the telescope at the end of your month. You will not be asked to return the telescope if you've had it for less than a month.

  6. You can pick up the telescope at any time during the month with costs as in item 1 to apply.

  7. You may keep the telescope for a maximum of one year.

Have fun with the `scope and thanks for supporting our Centre!
