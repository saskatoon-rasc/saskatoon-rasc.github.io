Welcome back to SSSP for 2012!

In 2001, Vance Petriew discovered a comet at the Saskatchewan Summer Star Party. In 2012, the comet returns to the skies! Vance is our Fr. Lucian Kemble Memorial Lecture speaker this year, talking about the original discovery and its return this year.

We will be back in the Meadows Campground! Details on the changes in the campground, including the new powered sites and the Service Centre, will be available soon.
